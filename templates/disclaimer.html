Health and Safety Notice

Welcome to PosturePal, a tool designed to help you maintain better posture while using your computer. Before proceeding, please read and acknowledge the following important information:

  • Not a Medical Device: PosturePal is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or health condition.
  • General Guidance Only: The posture detection and recommendations provided are for general guidance only and may not be suitable for individuals with specific medical conditions.
  • Consult Healthcare Professionals: If you have any existing health conditions, injuries, or concerns related to your posture or ergonomics, please consult with a qualified healthcare professional before using this application.
  • Privacy: PosturePal uses your camera to detect posture. All processing is done locally on your device, and no video data is stored or transmitted to external servers.
  • Take Breaks: Remember to take regular breaks regardless of what the application suggests. No posture monitoring tool replaces the need for regular movement and breaks from screen time.

System Requirements

For optimal performance, PosturePal requires:

  • A working webcam with clear visibility of your upper body
  • Adequate lighting in your workspace
  • A modern web browser with JavaScript enabled
  • Permission to access your camera
